United Philbrokers, Inc.

We are a group of professional real estate service practitioners leading the real estate industry by implementing R.A. 9646, the RESA Law, in terms of information, education and service. R.A. 9646 An act regulating the practice of real estate service in the Philippines creating for the purpose a professional regulatory board of real estate service, appropriating funds therefore and for other purposes.

Our Mission

To help ordinary Filipinos and Overseas Filipino Workers make informed and guided real estate acquisitions and financial conditions that go beyond one-time deals and geared toward preserving and maximizing the hard-earned cash of clients.

Our Vision

The Preferred Service - Focus on becoming a value conscious, real estate marketing company that offers the best deals for Filipinos here and abroad.
